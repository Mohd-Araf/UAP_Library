Library Membership Activation – UAP Library

  • Submit completed membership form (available at circulation desk or online)
  • Provide required documents:
    • 1 passport-size photo
    • Valid UAP ID
    • Active email and contact number
  • Verify student/faculty/staff status
  • Library account is created and activated
  • Borrowing privileges and access to library resources are granted
  • Member is responsible for all items issued on their ID
