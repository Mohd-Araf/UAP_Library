Library Circulation & Borrowing Policy – UAP Library

  • Only registered members may borrow materials from the library.
  • Borrowing limits and loan durations vary by user type (student, faculty, staff).
  • Borrowers must return materials by the due date to avoid fines.
  • Library resources must be handled with care; damage or loss will incur replacement costs.
  • Renewals may be requested unless the item is on hold for another user.
  • All borrowed items must be returned before clearance or graduation.
  • The library reserves the right to update borrowing privileges as needed.
